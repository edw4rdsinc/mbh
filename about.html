Meet the people behind My Benefits Help.

Friendly, knowledgeable, and ready to make your benefits experience easier — from anywhere in the country.

Our Mission: Simple, Human-Centered Benefits, Nationwide

MBH was built on a simple premise: benefits should be easy to understand and accessible to everyone. We achieve this by focusing on what truly matters: listening first, providing clear answers, and following through until every issue is resolved.

From anywhere in the country, we provide the responsive, human-centered support that your clients and employees deserve.
